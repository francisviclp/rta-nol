Frequently Asked Questions

What is Nol Plus?

Nol Plus is new loyalty and rewards program for Nol card users, where they can earn points and get many benefits.

Joining the program

I have a Silver Nol card (anonymous card), how can I join the NOL+ program?

For anonymous cards: You can start by personalizing your Nol card by logging in to the Nol website and following the process defined on there. Once your card personalization has been approved in 5 working days, you will be automatically enrolled into the program.

I have a personalized Silver / Gold card, how can I join NOL+ program?

For personalized cards: Personalized Silver or Gold NOL card users will be enrolled to the program automatically, and the system will start counting their points based on what they spend, starting from 1st January 2018.

I have a Nol Blue card (for adults, students or seniors), how can I join NOL+ program?

Nol Blue card users will be enrolled into the program automatically, and the system will start counting their points based on what they spend, starting from 1st January 2018. Nol Blue cards (adults, students and seniors) aren’t required to personalize their cards, and they will be enrolled into the program automatically.

I have a personalized Silver / Gold card, how can I join NOL+ program?

Personalized Silver or Gold Nol card users will be enrolled into the program automatically and the system will start counting their points based on what they spend, starting 1st January 2018.

I have more than one personalized Nol card - can I add all the cards to participate in the program?

Yes, all your personalized cards will be enrolled into the program automatically, and the system will start counting the points based on what they spend, starting from 1st January 2018.

I Have Emirates NBD Go4it card, do I need to personalize my card to join the program?

Emirates NBD Go4it cards will be enrolled into the program automatically; no personalization is required.

Earning and Spending Points

How do I earn Nol plus points?

You will earn 1 Nol Plus point for every AED 1 spent when you spend on your Nol Card. This means that if you spend AED 100, you will earn 100 points.

Can I earn Nol plus points if I pay for day-to-day spends like groceries and food and beverages with my Nol plus card?

Yes. The more you use your Nol card, the more Nol Plus points you earn. You can use your Nol card to spend on RTA services including Dubai metro, tram, bus, Dubai taxi, intercity bus, water taxi and parking; and at over 2,500 retail outlets including grocery stores and food and beverages outlets.

When are my Nol Plus points credited to my account?

The Nol Plus points you earn through your Nol card will be reflected in your account 9 days after you have earned them.

What can I spend my Nol Plus points for?

In the coming months, Nol Plus will launch across Dubai with even more offers, so you can also redeem all the Nol Points you’re now earning. To be able to redeem Nol plus points, you will need to register yourself on the RTA website and add your Nol Card to your account. Please look out for our upcoming offers.

For how long are my Nol Plus points valid?

Points earned prior to redemption becoming available will be valid for one year after redemption becomes available. Once redemption becomes available, your Nol Plus points will be valid for one year from the date they are posted to your account.

What services does the Nol Plus Member Service Centre provide?

The Nol Plus Member Service Centre consists of a dedicated team of professionals who will assist you with your queries regarding the Nol Plus program and your membership.

How do I become a registered member of the Nol Plus program?

To become a registered member of the Nol Plus program and enjoy full benefits such as being able to redeem points, you will need to register yourself on the RTA website and add your Nol Card to your account. Please look out for our upcoming offers and redemption options.

How can I find out my Nol Plus points?

You can check your Nol Plus points balance on the website, www.nolplus.ae. You will need to enter your Nol card tag ID to find out how many points you have in your Nol Plus account.

How can I find change my language preference / display on the Nol Plus website?

You can switch your language preference by updating your data preference at RTA; you can also:
- Access the English version of the page at: www.nolplus.ae/en
- Access the Arabic version of the page at: www.nolplus.ae/ar
